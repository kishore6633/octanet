filter_alt A filter in a photo editor changes how a photo looks by adjusting colors, tones, and textures. Filters in image editing software apply predefined effects to alter the appearance of photos, enhancing colors, and adjusting lighting for creative adjustments.
crop The crop tool in image editing lets you select and cut out specific parts of an image to adjust its composition or focus on key elements, adjusting its composition for improved focus and visual impact.
compare Enhance" in image editing involves adjusting brightness, contrast, sharpness, and color to improve clarity and visual appeal. It aims to refine and optimize the quality of images for better presentation or technical requirements.
background_replace A background remover is a tool that isolates the main subject of an image from its background, creating a transparent backdrop. It is used in graphic design and image editing to seamlessly integrate subjects into different contexts or compositions.
title Adding text to photos enhances storytelling, guiding viewers through visual narratives with clarity and emotion. Whether bold and impactful or subtle and nuanced, text overlays can elevate the meaning and context of an image.
photo_frame Applying a frame to a photo can give it a polished and professional look, emphasizing its subject and adding a touch of style. Frames can range from simple borders that draw attention to the image to intricate designs that complement its content, enhancing overall visual appeal.
